## 🎨 Image Recommendations

### **Service Images (800x600px each):**

1. **residential.jpg** 
   - Clean, modern living room or bedroom
   - Bright and welcoming
   - Shows cleanliness and comfort

2. **commercial.jpg**
   - Professional office space
   - Clean desks and conference room
   - Business atmosphere

3. **airbnb.jpg**
   - Well-made bed with fresh linens
   - Neat, organized space
   - Hospitality feel

4. **custom.jpg**
   - Cleaning equipment or supplies
   - Professional cleaner at work
   - Customization concept

5. **post-renovation.jpg**
   - Construction cleanup
   - Before/after transformation
   - Dust removal, deep clean

6. **move-in-out.jpg**
   - Empty, spotless room
   - Clean windows and floors
   - Fresh start feeling
